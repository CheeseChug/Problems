Stands are supernatural entities that belong to one person, referred to as a stand user and give the user supernatural powers. Stands are only visible to other stand users but can still physically affect non-stand users.

Stands can be awakened to or the power can be forcibly given to normal people through the use of a stand arrow or stand disc.
